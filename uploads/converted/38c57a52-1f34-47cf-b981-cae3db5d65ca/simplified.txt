Coverage matrix must have a 1 only if the outlet actually covers that zone; wrong matrix = wrong answer

Nurse Scheduling

The mod-7 rule: nurse starting Thu covers Thu,Fri,Sat,Sun,Mon тАФ NOT Tue,Wed. Encode this as 0s in matrix

Capital Budgeting

Exactly ONE option per plant (sum = 1, not тЙд 1); total cost тЙд budget; do NOT pick two options for one plant

M must be large enough to never bind when Y=1 (use max possible production qty as M)

Minimax Regret needs you to build the regret matrix first: regret = max in col - cell value

Cu and Co are per-unit costs, NOT total. Don't confuse selling price with profit per unit.

Cannot split projects. If budget runs out, skip the project тАФ no partial funding.
